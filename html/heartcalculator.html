12 Minute Walk/Run Test

Aerobic Capacity Test

Follow these instructions:

  1. Push yourself as hard as possible during the 12 minutes. Walk if necessary but endeavour to press through until the end of the 12 minutes.
  2. After 12 minutes has finished, record the distance.
